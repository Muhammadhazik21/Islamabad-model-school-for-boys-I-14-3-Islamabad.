School Modules

Introduction to Modules

This page highlights the core modules included within the school project. Each module is designed to enhance school management and support students, staff, and administrators through structured and efficient digital functionalities.

Core System Modules

1. Student Management Module
Handles student profiles, enrollment records, attendance tracking, school performance, and personal information. This module plays a central role in maintaining accurate student data for administrative use.
2. Faculty Management Module
Manages faculty profiles, subject assignments, schedules, and school responsibilities. This module supports smooth coordination between faculty and school departments.
3. Course & Curriculum Module
Provides complete details of courses, credit hours, outlines, and curriculum structure for different programs. Essential for school planning and program development.
4. Examination & Results Module
Automates exam scheduling, result compilation, grading, and transcript generation. Ensures accuracy, transparency, and timely school evaluation.
5. Admissions Module
Streamlines the process of new student admissions, application tracking, merit generation, and acceptance notifications. Supports both online and offline processes.
6. Library Management Module
Maintains records of borrowed books, availability, library inventory, membership data, and digital resources for students and faculty.
7. Finance & Fee Management Module
Handles student fee records, payment tracking, financial reports, invoices, and budgeting. Provides transparency in all financial operations.
