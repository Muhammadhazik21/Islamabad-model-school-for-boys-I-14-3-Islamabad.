Future Tracks - I-14/3 school

Introduction

Future Tracks at I-14/3 school is designed to guide students in shaping their professional journeys. It bridges school learning with practical career preparation, offering mentorship, internships, and real-world experiences to ensure every student is ready for success in their chosen field.

Main Purpose

The primary purpose of Future Tracks is to prepare students for meaningful careers by providing resources, guidance, and structured opportunities. It focuses on career exploration, skill development, and connecting students with industry professionals.

Main Objectives

  • Provide mentorship and guidance for career planning.
  • Offer access to internships, workshops, and networking events.
  • Develop practical skills for professional success.
  • Support informed decision-making regarding career choices.
  • Empower students to build fulfilling and sustainable careers.

Scope of Future Tracks

The scope includes career counseling, skill-building programs, internship placements, industry collaborations, and job preparation activities. Students can explore diverse fields, develop portfolios, and gain experience through hands-on projects and mentorship.

Key Features

  • Mentorship from faculty and industry experts.
  • Structured internship and placement programs.
  • Skill-building workshops and professional development.
  • Networking opportunities with professionals and alumni.
  • Resources to guide students in career exploration and planning.
